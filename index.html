On accuracy: the zenith's coordinates (RA = Local Sidereal Time, Dec = latitude) are exact for the date, time and location entered. The constellation shown is found by nearest-center matching against the 88 constellations' approximate midpoints, not the official IAU boundary polygons — reliable for most points, but it can occasionally call it differently right at a constellation border.
